Details of AIR FORCE



AIR FORCE is a range of athletic shoes made by Nike that began with Air Force 1.It was created by designer Bruce Kilgore and was the first basketball shoe to use Nike's "Air" technology. Nike Air is our iconic innovation that uses pressurized air in a durable, flexible membrane to provide lightweight cushioning.
